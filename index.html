Distance in Two Dimensions

The distance \( d \) between two points (x1, y1) and (x2, y2) in a 2D plane is calculated using the formula:

d = sqrt{(x2 - x1)2 + (y2 - y1)2}

Distance in Three Dimensions

The distance \( d \) between two points (x1, y1, z1) and (x2, y2, z2) in a 3D space is calculated using the formula:

d = sqrt{(x2 - x1)2 + (y2 - y1)2 + (z2 - z1)2}
